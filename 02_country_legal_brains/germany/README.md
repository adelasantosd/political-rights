## Key legal features to document (preliminary notes)

- Federal structure: Bund + 16 Länder; significant health competences at Länder level.
- Legal system: civil law tradition; German official language.
- Treaty incorporation: dualist system — treaties require implementing legislation (Zustimmungsgesetz) for domestic effect; once transformed, have rank of federal statute.
- IHR-relevant statute: *Infektionsschutzgesetz* (IfSG) of 20 July 2000, substantially amended in 2020–2021 (COVID-19 response).
- Health competence: Bund legislates framework; Länder implement and administer.
- Points of entry: major international airports (Frankfurt, Munich, etc.) and extensive land borders.
