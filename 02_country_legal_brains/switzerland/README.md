## Key legal features to document (preliminary notes)

- Federal structure: Confederation + 26 Cantons with significant autonomy in health matters.
- Legal system: civil law tradition; German, French, Italian, and Romansh official languages.
- Treaty incorporation: monist system — ratified treaties are directly applicable domestic law.
- IHR-relevant statute: *Epidemiengesetz* (EpG) / *Loi sur les épidémies* (LEp) of 28 September 2012, in force since 2016.
- Health competence: shared between Confederation (legislation, international coordination) and Cantons (implementation, health services).
- Points of entry: international airports (Zurich, Geneva, Basel) and ground crossings.
